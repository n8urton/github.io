Encypted Cross Platform Notes

2019-09-08
#pgp  #org  #encryption 
Challenges I’ve tried multiple note taking solutions, including OneNote, Dropbox, Quip, GDrive, Evernote, .txt files, cherrytree, and others. They all work great in some ways, fall short in others. None work platform independent. Requirements Portability Accessible from any machine with or without a browser No service, software, or OS lock in Configure what notes are synced and what notes are only accessible locally Private I shouldn’t have to agree to have them indexed by a 3rd party End to end encryption Nice to haves vi keybindings Rich formatting Export to different formats, including markdown and pdf Same environment on any workstation Works offline Enter Spacemacs + Org Mode + GnuPG Spacemacs is emacs pre-configured with a host of plug-ins, including emacs-evil, and extensible vi layer that add vi keybindings.
